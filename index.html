Hon'ble Health Minister Dr. Harsh Vardhan Ji's Message

1. I am pleased to know that advance healthcare foundation of India is organising a national level symposium titled "Adolescence - A Period of Bumps and Humps" wherein leading schools from Delhi NCR are participating

2. Students in the age group of adolescence are prone to a wide range of health issues and mental stress. Adolescence is the most criticle phase in a person's life, it is the age when one paves the path to become responsible future citizens, deliberations at the symposium by the experts including leading doctors will definitely benefit the participating students and all the stake holders present at the event.

3. I am glad to know that this flagship symposium will be taken to other cities to sensitise the burning issues faced by Adolescence. It is one of the best health care training centers in India.

4. I convey my best wishes to the organisers for the success of the symposium and make it foundation for health care quality.

A FEW WORDS ABOUT AHCF

Advance Health Care Foundation aims to create medical awareness about recent advancements made in the medical field and provide the facilities and discuss about advantages of the advancements. The organisation has intended to work on:

e-healthcare
Deceased organ donation and transplantation
Tele-medicine
Futuristic medicine
Online latest news related to healthcare & medicine

To achieve the goal of providing advance healthcare facilities, the organisation will conduct training sessions about healthcare, seminars with involvement of renowned doctors and planning to give online training in the near future.
Keeping pace with rapid growth of ICT, our foundation will take part of the digital India mission, making full use of mobile phones, computers and internet in the medical field in a very efficient and productive way.
